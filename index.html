DELIVERING THE WEP WILL:

  • Improve access to entertainment and sporting events and experiences for local residents of Toowoomba;
  • Increase liveability of the Toowoomba region and enhanced quality of life of its residents;
  • Deliver greater visitation to the Toowoomba region;
  • Attract new major national and international level motorsport events to Queensland and regional Australia;
  • Drive development and growth of Queensland’s and Australia’s motorsport industry;
  • Grow economic activity across the region;
  • Promote of improved road safety, particularly for regional Queensland.
